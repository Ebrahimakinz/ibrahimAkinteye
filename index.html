How it started...

I'm a civil engineering graduate with a background in project managagement, and my passion for data-driven decision-making and technology led me to explore other interests of mine. While working on the UX design of a mobile application, the fun I had analyzing the outcome of the research encouraged me even more. I realized I love to extract insights from data and share my findings. By using a bunch of online resources to learn, I started building projects to apply the new skills. I have worked on projects using Microsoft Excel, SQL, R, and visualization tools like Tableau and PowerBI, and I am open to collaborating on projects that improve peoples lives, make businesses better, and aid my growth.

When I am not working on a dataset, I spend some time researching how to improve the user experience of some mobile apps, watch football, and see what is trending in the data world. !
